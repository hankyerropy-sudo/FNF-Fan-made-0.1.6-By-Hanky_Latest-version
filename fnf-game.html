HOW TO PLAY

🎮 CONTROLS

📱 Mobile — Tap the 4 arrow buttons at the bottom

⌨️ PC — Arrow Keys or WASD

⏸ Pause — ESC key or the ⏸ button

🎵 HOW TO PLAY

  • ▸ Notes move toward the receptors
  • ▸ Press the matching arrow when note hits receptor zone
  • ▸ Hit = BF HP up  ·  Miss = BF HP down
  • ▸ BF bar reaches zero → GAME OVER!
  • ▸ Survive the whole song → WIN!

    • ❤️ HEALTH BAR

  • ▸ Left 🔵 Blue = Opponent HP
  • ▸ Right 🔴 Pink = BF HP
  • ▸ Center dot moves toward the winner
  • ▸ Winning character emoji glows!

    • ⭐ RATINGS

  • 🔵 SICK! — Perfect hit (+100 pts)
  • 🟢 GOOD — Good timing (+75 pts)
  • 🟡 BAD — Slightly off (+25 pts)
  • 🔴 MISS — Too late / ghost tap (−HP)

    • 📊 TIME BAR

  • ▸ Shows song progress at the top
  • ▸ Green → Yellow → Pink as song ends
  • ▸ Timer shows elapsed time
